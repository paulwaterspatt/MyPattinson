Registration

Step 1:

From the main Pattinson.co.uk website, hover the mouse over the "My Pattinson" link and choose Register. This will bring the customer to the main registration page.



Step 2:

Once all the fields are completed, click Register.

This will now display the main customer dashboard:



Step 3:

You can now scroll down to show all areas of the customer's journey, including any offers or viewings related to buying a property.

Details of properties the customer has registered to sell, rent, or let as a landlord are also displayed here. Offers can be viewed per property category.

On the left-hand side, the customer can access details of viewings, offers, and other interactions. Verification steps are part of this journey and ensure data and activity accuracy.




Verification

By clicking on the My Details section on the left hand side, customers can now complete all areas and confirm marketing preferences.

Please note that once the Date of Birth and Drivers License details are entered, this will initiate an email to the Team and Auction Admin for them to complete the AML check.

Customers cannot bid until this process has been Passed.





EchoSign

When customers are sent an agreement for signature, below is what they receive in their inbox:

They need to click the blue "Review and Sign" button


Step 1

The customer must click Continue before they are able to complete the agreement



Step 2

The customer can now complete all fields in Blue, however there will be required fields which must be completed. These are shown at the bottom



Step 3

Signing the agreement can be done in a number of ways:

  • Type a signature (this will make it look like a written signature)
  • Draw this will let the customer use their finger to draw a signature if using an Ipad etc
  • Image this allows the customer to upload an image of a signature (this is never used)
  • Mobile this sends a link to the customers mobile phone to enable them to draw a signature

Once all required fields have been completed, the document will return the document to the sender and display this screen to the customer:
